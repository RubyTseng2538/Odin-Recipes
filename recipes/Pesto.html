Pesto

Description

Pesto is a flavorful sauce made from fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, and olive oil. It can be used as a pasta sauce, spread, or dip.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
  • 2/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/3 cups nutritional yeast
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Step 1

Place pine nuts in a skillet over medium heat; cook, stirring or shaking the pan often, until lightly toasted.

Step 2

Place toasted pine nuts, olive oil, garlic, nutritional yeast, and basil in a food processor; blend until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
